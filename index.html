About Me My name is Jason Harris and I grew up in Mount Olive, New Jersey. I'm currently pursuing my bachelors degree in Information Technology at NJIT. I have a specialization of network security and have goals of becoming a system administrator or network administrator.

Get to know me!

My journey with technology began at a young age. Growing up, I was fascinated by the endless possibilities technology offered. Whether it was taking apart gadgets or learning new software, my passion for technology was clear from the start. As I got older, this passion grew. I learned various programming languages, built my own computers, and explored the expanding world of I.T. Now, I am a senior at the New Jersey Institute of Technology, studying Information Technology. I focus on network security and system administration. I have hands-on experience in fixing security issues, managing SIEM systems, and providing IT support.
